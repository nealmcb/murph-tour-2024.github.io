Sharon Guli
and the Rocky Mountain Vintage Dancers
have been invited to perform again on

Michael Martin Murphey's

2024 Cowboy Christmas Concert Tour!





November 21 - December 22, 2024
are the dates Sharon and the Dance Team will be joining the Tour.
Please join us!
Feel free to come in Victorian and Old West costumes -
Michael Martin Murphey loves it when you do.





 

Click on link below to purchase tickets!
https://www.michaelmartinmurphey.com/tour

NOTE #1: A few of the dates are listed on his website as Christmas Balls (Dec. 12, 14, and 20), where there will be a dance floor, too. Especially consider attending the original Cowboy Christmas Ball on Dec.12 in Anson, TX!

NOTE #2: There are four concerts in Colorado, in Colorado Springs, Grand Junction, and Fort Collins! Please come join us!



Michael Martin Murphey with Amy Grant "Wildfire"
Michael Martin Murphey & the Rio Grande Band - "Wildfire"

Michael Martin Murphey, an American singer-songwriter, has six gold albums and is a multiple Grammy nominee. He is known for his hit singles which include "Wildfire," "Carolina in the Pines," What's Forever For," "A Long Line of Love," "Geronimo's Cadillac," "Cowboy Logic," and his long line of highly successful Cowboy Songs albums.



Come watch Sharon Guli perform Old-Time Cowboy dances during Michael Martin Murphey's concerts taking place between Nov. 21- Dec. 22, along with her fellow dancers. Some concerts feature two dancers, some four.
 
A select few of the concerts will feature dance floors to turn the evening
into a Cowboy Christmas Ball. At these events, there will be room for you to join Sharon for dances such as the Virginia Reel, Schottische, and more!

Cowboy and Victorian costuming encouraged but not required.
